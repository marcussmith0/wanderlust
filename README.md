# Wanderlust

Our app will allow users to create, update, delete, and read posts which will consist mostly of pictures and images of different users travel experience. The goal of our site is to be the Facebook, Instagram, Twitter but specifically for travel. But the thing that sets our app at apart is the fact that our app sends you a list of recommendations based on your geolocation of places you should go. This could be resturants, museums, or other points of interest in your location. App will allow users to have followers, to comment on different posts, like/dislike comments, favorite the posts that they like.
